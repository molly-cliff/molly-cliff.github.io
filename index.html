About

I’m Molly, a second-year PhD student at the University of Cambridge, investigating the potential impact of climate change on the distribution of bacterial meningitis. My research uses statistical and geospatial methods to understand how varying climatic factors can affect the risk of outbreak occurrence.

I hope to use my background in infectious disease epidemiology to support global public health programs, focusing on integrating research into real-world practice.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy rock climbing, making art, and listening to music.

Research

Exploring how environmental and climatic factors influence infectious disease patterns, particularly bacterial meningitis, across Africa.
